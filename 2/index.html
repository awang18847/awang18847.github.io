Fun with Filters and Frequencies

Overview

The goal of this project is to explore the concepts of filters and frequency transformations in image processing. Through this project, we will use techniques such as finite difference operators, Gaussian filters, and hybrid image creation. We will also focus on sharpening and multiresolution blending to create visually interesting results.
